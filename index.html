❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Which are the most relevant books for an aspiring software engineer?

Some great books include "Clean Code" by Robert C. Martin, "The Pragmatic Programmer" by Andrew Hunt and David Thomas, and "Design Patterns" by Erich Gamma et al.

What is good about Power Learn Project?

Power Learn Project (PLP) is more than just a coding program — it’s a movement empowering young Africans to become world-class software developers. 🌍💻 Through free, high-quality training in programming, web development, and entrepreneurship, PLP gives learners practical skills that open doors to real opportunities. What makes PLP truly special is its focus on community: learners support one another, collaborate on projects, and grow together as problem-solvers and innovators. 🤝✨ With mentorship, hands-on projects, and access to global networks, PLP isn’t just teaching tech — it’s building Africa’s next generation of tech leaders. 🚀
